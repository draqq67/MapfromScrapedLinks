Welcome to Standard Security
  Standard Security are a family run, NSI GOLD accredited, security company based in Blyth, Northumberland.
Established in 1985 we, design, install, & maintain a full range of security systems throughout Northumberland, Tyne & Wear, Durham, Cleveland & the Scottish Borders
As we are a family run business we are able to focus on our customers and we are committed to giving them the care and attention to detail we feel they deserve.
We are NSI Gold accredited for the design, installation and maintenance of electronic security systems for intruder alarms, CCTV systems and access control. We are also CHAS & Constructionline accredited.
For further information about Standard Security, please do not hesitate to get in touch. We are always happy to help.
