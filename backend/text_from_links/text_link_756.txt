WHO WE ARE
Veterans Rebuilding Life is a registered, 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with a mission focused on assisting the victims of war, and humanitarian crises. Established in 2011, this grass roots charity was founded by American combat veterans of the global war on terror.
OUR MISSION
From left: VRL members Xenia and Christian Zamora, standing alongside Louis Costa.
Veterans Rebuilding Life members and supporters come from all branches of military service.
WHAT WE DO
Instead of replicating existing nonprofit services, we target humanitarian needs that lack support, and provide evidence based solutions that have a proven record of success.

HOW WE DO IT
Veterans Rebuilding Life programs and services are provided at no cost to those in need. These services are provided by a team of qualified and committed volunteers.

RECORD OF INTEGRITY
Veterans Rebuilding Life does not accept financial support or sponsorship from any special interest groups. Our volunteers carry out a mission that is made possible through the continued support of our shared community.

OUR SUPPORTERS
