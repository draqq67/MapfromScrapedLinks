ABOUT US
We are a leading underground precious metals company, focusing on the exploration, mining, processing and sale of gold and silver in the Americas.
Hochschild operates two underground epithermal deposits, one in the southwest of Peru and one in southern Argentina. We also operate the Mara Rosa open pit mine in Brazil.
