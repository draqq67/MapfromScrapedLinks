Welcome to ELITE Sports UK 
Elite Sports UK are the owners of ESC Arley and managers of ESC Coventry, ESC Nuneaton, ESC Kingsdwown and Daimler Green 3G Pitch. 
We are also the proud founders of ELITE Swimming ‘Pools to Schools’ with over 50 pop up pools across the uk to date. 
On our website you will be able to find out more information about our gyms and exercise classes, swim programmes including baby and toddler and adult sessions, birthday parties, holiday provision as well as our Elite Adventure section with our mobile climbing wall and outdoor adventure programmes.
