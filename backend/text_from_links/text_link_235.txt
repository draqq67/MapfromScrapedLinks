Corporate Imprints focuses on providing quality machine embroidery, embroidery design, screen-printed wearables and other promotional materials.
NOT JUST EMBROIDERY!
Our company was founded over 20 years ago in Denver, Colorado. We service accounts throughout the United States.
We offer state of the art in-house machine embroidery, keeping an eye on "Perfection" of finished goods.
We also provide screen printing, graphics consulting and design, and specialized imprinted promotional products (ASI Membership).

WHY CHOOSE CORPORATE IMPRINTS FOR YOUR EMBROIDERY OR PROMOTIONAL PRODUCT NEEDS?
That's easy! We have over twenty years of machine embroidery experience and we also have local, national, and international sourcing.
Choose us for any custom graphic design and digitizing you might have, and we can apply your digitized design to a wide range of apparel, t-shirts, caps, coats and leather jackets.
Corporate Imprints does all in-house embroidery with over 30 Tajima and Amaya embroidery sewing stations which allows for high production capabilities. We also offer custom screen printing and embossing.
If that's not enough, we also have over 500,000 promotional products offered through the Advertising and Specialty Institute (ASI) platform.
Additionally, for value or closeout requirements, we maintain direct offshore supplier relationships for quantity orders.
