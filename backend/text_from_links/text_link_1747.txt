At Maclaren Jones Design & Marketing, we have over 20 years’ experience
of relentlessly building successful brands.
We’re a boutique, design & marketing agency based in Knutsford, Cheshire, just south of Manchester with a portfolio of clients nationwide. We bring a refreshing, commercially-minded and results-driven approach to advertising, design and marketing in all its guises. We’ve got the skills and experience to rival big city players plus the agility and personality to allow us to feel like an extension of your team. It’s a winning combination and one that sees our clients sticking around for years.
We’re the ideas factory that you never knew you needed – strategy-focused yet unfaltering in our passion for delivering smart, creative solutions that get noticed. We build relationships that last – we slot right in but shake things up. Brimming with bright ideas, the word ‘no’ doesn’t feature in our vocabulary. We tackle challenges head on, helping clients to clear the creative brain fog and capture the attention and imagination of their target audience.
