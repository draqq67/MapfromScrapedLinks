About
MJ OBrien Design was established in Orange County, California in 1987 by Mary Jane O’Brien.
Mary Jane holds degrees and numerous industry awards in graphic design. With her team, the Company has
particular experience in web design, e-mail marketing, direct mail campaigns, print material development and
branding for retail, non-profits, and service business.
