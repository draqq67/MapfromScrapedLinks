Music Production and Creation, Film and Video Game Scoring, Soundtracks
Welcome! Fuseroom is owned and operated by pianist/keyboardist, composer, producer and sound engineer Alberto Rizzo Schettino.
The studio serves as Alberto's main location to score and produce original music for film, video games and multimedia, along with his music education projects, production and co-production of indie artists, recording, mixing, mastering, and more.
In the years, work done at Fuseroom has been licensed to companies such as Blizzard Entertainment, Riot Games, Ubisoft, Netflix, Audi, Fox News, Etihad Airways, and more.
